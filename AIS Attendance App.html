03 The Core Objective

Weekly Engagement Emails

The primary goal of the app is to automate and personalise communication, bridging the gap between school data and parent/student awareness.

For Parents

Clear visibility on their child's attendance.

For Students

Accountability and positive reinforcement.

For Admin

Zero manual effort required.

🔒

Safety first: All data uses Unique Identifiers in the links instead of usernames or school IDs.

04 Parent Communication

Celebrating Success.

When a student achieves 100% attendance in the last 7 days, the Attendance App celebrates this achievement!

This proactive approach builds a positive relationship between school and families, shifting from policing absence to celebrating presence.

05 Student Insights

Constructive Feedback.

For students with less than 100% attendance, the app provides a detailed breakdown of the impact.

  • Visualised loss: percentage dropped.
  • Real impact: minutes, hours, and lessons missed.
  • Actionable: encourages improvement next week.
